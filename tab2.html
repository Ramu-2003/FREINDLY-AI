Website Rules

Welcome to Doc-Locker! Below are the rules to ensure safe and secure usage of our file-locking platform:

  • Only authorized users with a valid PIN or password can upload, view, or delete files.
  • Do not share your pass-key with anyone to ensure your data remains private.
  • Files uploaded will be securely stored and only accessible with the pass-key.
  • Ensure you keep a backup of important files, as deletion is irreversible.
